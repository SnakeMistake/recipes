Lemon Berry Tart

Getting Started

This is a quick and easy way to make your fruit into something delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 lemons, squeezed and zested
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 eggs, separated
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/3 cup sugar
    • <1i>1/2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon tapioca
  • Powdered sugar
  • 1-2 pints of Fruit, including any of the following:
    • Blueberries
    • Sliced Peaches
    • Blackberries
    • Raspberries

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325.
  2. Rinse the berries and drain or slice larger fruit. Combine with 2 tablespoons of sugar and let the flavors develop while you prepare the pudding.
  3. Whisk egg yolks with lemon zest and juice. Whisk in flour salt and sugar until smooth. Whisk in butter and milk. Beat the egg whites until firm and gently fold them into the yolk mixture. Lightly combine the tapioca with the summer fruit.
  4. Place the fruit in a buttered casserole and pour the batter over the top. Bake until the pudding is lightly browned and cooked through (well raised in the middle), about 45-50 minutes. Serve warm or at room temperature, dusted with podered sugar.
